our story

Born from a tiny studio and a lot of good music

WhimsiCraft started in 2018 when Mara cleared out her garage, bought a secondhand wheel, and started throwing. What began as a weekend obsession became something people kept wanting to bring home.

We work exclusively with stoneware and earthenware, sourced from small clay suppliers. Everything is glazed, bisque fired, then glaze fired — twice through the kiln, minimum.

How each piece becomes itself

Every piece takes between 2 and 4 weeks from raw clay to wrapped and shipped.

①

Wedge & Centre

Clay is wedged by hand to remove air bubbles, then centred on the wheel.

②

Throw & Trim

Each form is pulled up from a single lump of clay, then trimmed leather-hard.

③

Bisque Fire

Pieces go through their first firing at ~1000°C, making them porous and ready to glaze.

④

Glaze & Fire

Hand-dipped or brushed glaze, then a final firing at cone 6. Done.
